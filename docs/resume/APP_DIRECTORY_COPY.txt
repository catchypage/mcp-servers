Description
================================================================================
Resume Builder helps you create, tailor, and improve professional resumes right inside ChatGPT. Open the app, pick one of 8 styles (Classic, Modern, Executive, Creative, Terminal, Sidebar, Swiss, Nature), fill in your details, and download a polished PDF. Use "vacancy" mode to tailor your resume for a specific job posting, or "improve" mode to enhance an existing resume with AI feedback.

Release Notes — required (may appear on app details page)
================================================================================
Bug fixes and other improvements.
- Open Resume Builder from chat to load the interactive widget after sign-in.
- Choose from 8 professional resume styles: Classic, Modern, Executive, Creative, Terminal, Sidebar, Swiss, Nature.
- Three modes: create a new resume, tailor for a specific vacancy, or improve an existing resume.
- Fill in personal info, experience, education, and skills in the inline form.
- Live preview of your resume as you type.
- Download your resume as a PDF via a secure short-lived token link.
- AI can pre-fill fields from your prompt (name, job title, style).
