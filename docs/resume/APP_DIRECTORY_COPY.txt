Description
================================================================================
Resume Booster helps you create, tailor, and improve professional resumes right inside ChatGPT. Open the app, pick one of 8 styles (Classic, Modern, Executive, Creative, Terminal, Sidebar, Swiss, Nature), fill in your details, and download a polished PDF. Use "vacancy" mode to tailor your resume for a specific job posting, or "improve" mode to enhance an existing resume with AI feedback.
