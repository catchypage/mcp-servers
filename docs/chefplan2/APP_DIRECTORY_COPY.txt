Calories, macros & recipe finder

================================================================================
Description
================================================================================
ChefPlan calculates your daily energy needs (BMR, TDEE, and macros) using the Mifflin-St Jeor equation and helps you find recipes with photos, ingredients, and step-by-step instructions. Enter your weight, height, age, gender, activity level, and goal to get a personalized calorie target. Search for recipes by name or get a random dish — all inside the ChatGPT widget. No sign-in required.

Release Notes — required (may appear on app details page)

Bug fixes and other improvements.
- Metabolism calculator: enter weight, height, age, gender, activity level, and goal to get BMR, TDEE, and recommended macros (protein, carbs, fat).
- Recipe finder: search by name or get a random recipe with photo, ingredients list, step-by-step instructions, and YouTube video link.
- Interactive widget with Calculator and Recipes tabs.
- Search results show a paginated list (10 per page) with thumbnails.
- Recalculate button on results screen to adjust parameters.
- No authentication required — works without sign-in.
