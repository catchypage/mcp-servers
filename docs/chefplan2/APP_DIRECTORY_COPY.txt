Description
================================================================================
ChefPlan calculates your daily energy needs (BMR, TDEE, and macros) using the Mifflin-St Jeor equation and helps you find recipes with photos, ingredients, and step-by-step instructions. Enter your weight, height, age, gender, activity level, and goal to get a personalized calorie target. Search for recipes by name or get a random dish — all inside the ChatGPT widget. No sign-in required.
