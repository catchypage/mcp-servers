Release Notes — required (may appear on app details page)
================================================================================
(Public text. Describe what this version of the app actually does today; update each release.)

Bug fixes and other improvements.
- Open Lang Coach from chat to load the inline widget after sign-in. The connector can include an optional focus topic (e.g. grammar); it is reflected in the assistant-visible message.
- Take an English placement test in the widget: multiple-choice questions, scored to a CEFR level (A1–C1) with a score breakdown by area.
- Your latest level and score are saved to your Lang Coach profile; you can retake the test from the home screen.
- Home screen shows your current band or invites you to start the test; header shows your avatar and a link to your account on the website.
- Choose light or dark theme and a supported UI language; settings persist on your profile.
- Daily practice, streaks, and vocabulary builder appear as “coming soon” and are not available in this release.
